# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters guide agile ceremonies, coach the team on agile principles, remove impediments, and foster continuous improvement. They work closely with the Project Manager to maintain flow and help teams self-organize effectively.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate agile ceremonies (daily standups, sprint planning, retrospectives, reviews)
- Coach team members on agile principles and practices
- Identify and remove impediments blocking team progress
- Foster a culture of continuous improvement and transparency
- Protect the team from external disruptions and distractions
- Collaborate with Project Manager on delivery coordination

### Goals
- Enable team self-organization and autonomy
- Improve team velocity and predictability over time
- Reduce waste and increase value delivery
- Build a high-trust, collaborative team environment

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standups and impediment resolution
- Sprint ceremonies and team retrospectives
- Coaching conversations with team members and stakeholders
- Collaboration with Project Manager on process improvements

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Project Manager**: Partner on delivery coordination, risk management, and stakeholder communication
- **Developers**: Coach on agile practices, remove blockers, facilitate collaboration
- **Product Manager**: Ensure backlog clarity and facilitate sprint planning
- **Technical Lead**: Coordinate on technical improvements and team development

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers are responsible for user research, prototyping, and ensuring usability standards. They create intuitive user experiences through research-driven design and iterative testing.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Ensure designs meet accessibility and usability standards
- Validate designs with users and iterate based on feedback
- Collaborate with Product Manager on user stories and requirements
- Work with Developers to ensure design implementation fidelity

### Goals
- Create intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Validate designs with real user feedback
- Maintain consistency across the product
- Reduce user friction and support issues

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critique sessions
- User research findings presentations
- Collaborative design sessions with Product Manager and Developers
- Usability test reports and recommendations

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborate during requirement definition and validate user needs
- **Developers**: Partner during implementation to ensure design fidelity
- **QA Lead**: Coordinate on usability testing and acceptance criteria
- **Business/Stakeholder Representative**: Present designs and gather feedback

---

## Technical Lead

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide technical guidance, review architecture decisions, and ensure code quality standards. They act as mentors for Developers and technical advisors to Product and Project Managers.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain technical architecture and standards
- Review critical design and implementation decisions
- Mentor Developers on best practices and code quality
- Conduct technical feasibility assessments
- Lead technical discussions and design reviews
- Coordinate with DevOps Engineer on infrastructure decisions

### Goals
- Maintain high technical quality and architectural consistency
- Build technical capability across the development team
- Reduce technical debt and improve maintainability
- Enable scalable and reliable system design

### Typical Communication
- Architecture design docs and technical RFCs
- Code reviews and technical mentoring sessions
- Feasibility discussions with Product Manager
- Technical roadmap planning with engineering leadership

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Mentor on technical growth and review critical code
- **Product Manager**: Advise on technical feasibility and trade-offs
- **Project Manager**: Provide technical estimates and identify technical risks
- **DevOps Engineer**: Collaborate on infrastructure and deployment architecture

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers support build, deployment, monitoring, and infrastructure automation. They enable reliable and efficient software delivery through CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure as code.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain CI/CD pipelines
- Manage infrastructure provisioning and configuration
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Automate deployment and release processes
- Ensure system reliability, security, and performance
- Partner with Developers and QA on testing and deployment

### Goals
- Enable fast, reliable, and automated deployments
- Maintain high system availability and performance
- Reduce manual operations and toil
- Improve observability and incident response

### Typical Communication
- Infrastructure design docs and runbooks
- Deployment notifications and release coordination
- Post-incident reviews and system health reports
- Automation planning with Developers and QA Lead

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Support build and deployment needs, provide tooling
- **QA Lead**: Collaborate on test automation and deployment pipelines
- **Technical Lead**: Partner on infrastructure architecture decisions
- **Project Manager**: Coordinate on release planning and risk management

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads own the overall test strategy, coordinate manual and automated testing, and ensure quality standards are met. They work across the team to define acceptance criteria and quality gates.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test strategy and quality standards
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Partner with Product Manager and Developers on acceptance criteria
- Lead bug triage and quality reviews
- Implement test automation frameworks and practices
- Track quality metrics and test coverage

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Shift testing left in the development process
- Increase test automation coverage
- Reduce defect escape rate and production issues

### Typical Communication
- Test plans and quality reports
- Bug triage sessions and defect reviews
- Quality metrics and test coverage reports
- Collaboration with team on acceptance criteria

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Partner on unit testing, test automation, and bug fixes
- **Product Manager**: Define acceptance criteria and quality requirements
- **DevOps Engineer**: Collaborate on CI/CD integration and test automation
- **UX Designer**: Coordinate on usability testing and user acceptance testing

---

## Business/Stakeholder Representative

### Role Summary
Business/Stakeholder Representatives ensure stakeholder requirements are understood and addressed. They provide business context, review deliverables, and maintain feedback loops with the product team.

### Responsibilities
- Represent business and stakeholder interests in project discussions
- Provide business requirements and success criteria
- Review deliverables and provide feedback
- Validate that solutions meet business needs
- Communicate progress and outcomes to stakeholders
- Help prioritize features based on business value

### Goals
- Ensure project outcomes align with business objectives
- Maintain stakeholder engagement and satisfaction
- Provide timely feedback to guide development
- Bridge communication between business and technical teams

### Typical Communication
- Requirements gathering and clarification sessions
- Progress reviews and demo participation
- Stakeholder updates and feedback summaries
- Business value and ROI discussions

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Manager**: Collaborate on prioritization and requirements definition
- **Project Manager**: Provide feedback on progress and coordinate stakeholder communication
- **UX Designer**: Review designs and provide business perspective
- **Developers**: Participate in demos and provide acceptance feedback
